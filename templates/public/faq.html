Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our AI Job Matching platform.

Our platform uses advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) to read your uploaded PDF CV. It extracts your skills, experience, and education, and compares them against thousands of active job posts to calculate a real-time matching percentage score.
When you view a job detail page, our AI compares the job's required skills with the skills found in your CV. If you are missing any crucial skills, the system highlights them as areas for improvement, helping you know exactly what to learn to get hired.
