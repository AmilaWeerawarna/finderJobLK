# FinderJobLK - AI-Powered Job Matching Portal 🚀

Welcome to **FinderJobLK**, an advanced, intelligent job board platform designed to revolutionize the hiring process in Sri Lanka. 

Unlike traditional job portals, FinderJobLK leverages **Artificial Intelligence and Natural Language Processing (NLP)** to automatically analyze CVs, calculate match scores, and provide actionable skill gap analysis for job seekers. It also offers a seamless, automated shortlisting experience for employers.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 👨‍💻 For Job Seekers
* **Smart AI CV Matching:** Upload your PDF CV, and our NLP engine will read it like a human, extracting your skills, education, and experience.
* **Real-time Match Scoring:** Instantly see a percentage score indicating how well your profile matches a specific job post.
* **Skill Gap Analysis:** The system highlights the exact skills you are missing for a particular job, helping you focus your learning path.
* **Modern UI:** A beautiful, dark-themed Glassmorphism interface for a smooth user experience.

### 🏢 For Employers
* **Automated Candidate Shortlisting:** No more manual resume screening. The AI automatically ranks applicants based on their match score.
* **Comprehensive Dashboard:** Manage Active Jobs, Save Drafts, Reactivate Closed Jobs, and monitor real-time statistics (Total Applications, Profile Views).
* **Rich Text Job Editor:** Easily format job duties and requirements using the integrated Quill.js rich text editor.
* **Secure Document Handling:** Safely view and download candidate CVs directly from the dashboard.

### 🛡️ For Administrators
* **Employer Verification System:** New employer accounts are placed in a "Pending" state until an admin verifies their company details, ensuring platform safety and authenticity.

---

## 🛠️ Technology Stack

* **Backend:** Python, Flask
* **Database:** MongoDB (PyMongo)
* **AI & NLP:** Natural Language Toolkit (NLTK), Deep Text Embedding models
* **Frontend:** HTML5, CSS3 (Custom Dark Theme, CSS Variables, Glassmorphism), Vanilla JavaScript
* **Other Tools:** Quill.js (Rich Text Editor), FontAwesome
